About Us

Based in Chicago, Illinois, Little Lemon is a family-owned Mediterranean restaurant focused on traditional recipes served with a modern twist.

The chefs draw inspiration from Italian, Greek, and Turkish culture and rotate a seasonal menu of 12-15 dishes.

The restaurant offers a warm, relaxed atmosphere with moderate prices, perfect for lunch, dinner, or a weekend meal.

Little Lemon is owned by two brothers, Mario and Adrian, who moved to the United States to pursue their shared dream of opening a restaurant.

Mario brings family recipes and culinary experience from Italy, while Adrian handles the restaurant’s marketing and creative direction.
